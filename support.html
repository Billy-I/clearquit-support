Support

Practical help with ClearQuit and ClearQuit Pro.

A purchase is not showing

Open ClearQuit, go to Settings and select Restore purchases. Make sure the device is signed in to the Apple Account used for the original purchase. Restoring does not charge you again.

Manage or cancel ClearQuit Pro

Open ClearQuit Settings and select Manage subscription, or open iOS Settings, select your Apple Account, then select Subscriptions.

Reminders are not appearing

Check that reminders are enabled in ClearQuit Settings and that notifications are allowed for ClearQuit in iOS Settings. Focus modes can also silence notification alerts.

Your tracking data

Quit plans, check-ins and craving records are currently stored on your device. Deleting ClearQuit also deletes that local app data. iCloud sync is not currently available.

Health support

ClearQuit cannot provide medical or emergency help. Contact a qualified healthcare professional or a local stop-smoking service for clinical guidance. Contact local emergency services if you are in immediate danger.
